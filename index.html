About this event

UTADA Innovation Fair 2020 is a computer programming(coding)-based application design competition. There are three topics of this event: Artificial Intelligence (AI), University Life, and Post-Epidemic Era. Participants will brainstorm through the provided themes and create designs and applications within a limited time. Students with creativity and interest in computer programming will have an opportunity to communicate with elites from various industries and to showcase their talents.
